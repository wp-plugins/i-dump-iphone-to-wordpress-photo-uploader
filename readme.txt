This plugin allows you to upload photos or images from visitors or yourself directly into your wordpress i-Dump gallery using the i-Dump for windows or WP-Dump iphone app.

== Description ==

i-Dump is a great and fast plugin for WordPress blogs to upload images/photos directly from your Windows desktop into your WordPress i-Dump gallery.
Once this plugin is installed you can provide a personal username and password or you can use it in internal group purposes. By this it turns your blog into a social photo blog by letting users share their images or photos!
Test it out at http:/www.i-dump.info !

== Installation ==

1. Upload `i-Dump` folder into the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Open de i-Dump settings at the admin menu bar.
4. Make sure your server settings are allowed to have write permission (see FAQ)
5. Download your iPhone app at https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/wp-dump/id460989802?l=nl&ls=1&mt=8

if you need support how to install plugins, please checkout  
http://codex.wordpress.org/Managing_Plugins

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Compatible with i-Dump For Windows & WP-Dump 1.2(iPhone)at the iTunes Appstore =

- A : I cannot find the iPhone app in my store, why ?
- Q : At the moment i am working on a new version and launched the Windows Desktop application
- Q : My pics don't show up, whats happening?
- A : Make sure your wp-content/uploads/i-dump-uploads/ folder have permission to write.
- Q : How do i allow public uploads from my blog visitors?
- A : Leave the Username/password fields empty, your visitor only need to fill in your domain.
- Q : How do i show my gallery photos on my blog?
- A : You can use the [idumpgallery] tag anywhere on your page or posts to show your gallery.
- Q : Can i delete the photos from management cause gallery size aint less after removing?
- A : No, in this version you will always keep a backup of your photos.
- Q : Do i need an iPhone myself to allow other to upload their photos?
- A : No, you don't need an iPhone to let iPhone users upload their photos into your blog.
- Q : Can i use this plugin with more then 2 iPhone users?
- A : Yes you can let unlimited users upload to your blog, unless they have the WP-Dump iphone app.
- Q : Where do i find the WP-Dump iPhone app ?
- A : You can find the WP-Dump iPhone app at https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/wp-dump/id460989802?l=nl&ls=1&mt=8
- Q : My text captions does not appear under the photos, what is wrong?
- A : If there is no caption send from your iPhone, the date will automatically appear.
- Q : I don't see my question above, where can i contact the developer?
- A : You can contact the developer at www.i-dump.info for all your questions 
- Q : I wish to show my gallery that they appear big in a lightbox.
- A : i-Dump Gallery can be used perfectly with Wordpress Fancybox available at http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/fancybox-for-wordpress/

== About i-Dump ==

i-Dump is very simple plugin to adapt Windows destop or iPhone users with your WordPress Blog. Once they installed i-Dump for Windows or WP-Dump (at the appstore) they will be compatible to upload their interests at your blog. You can manage users groups with this plugin, by that you can allow public acces too. To simple show your gallery on your pages or posts you can use the [idumpgallery] tag.
