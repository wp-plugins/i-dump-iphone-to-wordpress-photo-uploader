This plugin allows you to upload photos or images from iPhone visitors directly into your wordpress i-Dump gallery using the WP-Dump iphone app.

== Description ==

i-Dump is a simple and fast plugin for WordPress blogs to upload directly their iPhone photos into your blog.
Once its installed you can provide a personal username and password or you can use it in internal group purposes. By this it turns your blog into a social mobile photo blog!

== Installation ==

1. Upload `i-Dump` folder into the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Open de i-Dump settings at the admin menu bar.

if you need support how to install plugins, please checkout  
http://codex.wordpress.org/Managing_Plugins

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Use WP-Dump 1.1 (iphone) From the iTunes Appstore =

- Q : How do i allow public uploads from my blog visitors?
- A : Leave the Username/password fields empty, your visitor only need to fill in your domain.
- Q : How do i show my gallery photos on my blog?
- A : You can use the [idumpgallery] tag anywhere on your page or posts to show your gallery.
- Q : Do i need an iPhone myself to allow other to upload their photos?
- A : No, you don't need an iPhone to let iPhone users upload their photos into your blog.
- Q : Can i use this plugin with more then 2 iPhone users?
- A : Yes you can let unlimited users upload to your blog, unless they have the WP-Dump iphone app.
- Q : Where do i find the WP-Dump iPhone app ?
- A : You can find the WP-Dump iPhone app at http://itun.es/iF78p4
- Q : I don't see my question above, where can i contact the developer?
- A : You can contact the developer at www.i-dump.info for all your questions

== About i-Dump ==

i-Dump is very simple plugin to adapt iPhone users with your WordPress Blog. Once they installed WP-Dump at the appstore they will be compatible to upload their interests at your blog. You can manage users groups with this plugin, by that you can allow public acces too. To simple show your gallery on your pages or posts you can use the [idumpgallery] tag.
