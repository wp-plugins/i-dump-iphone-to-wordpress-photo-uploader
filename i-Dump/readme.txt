This plugin let you upload photos or images from your iPhone directly into your wordpress i-Dump gallery.

== Description ==

i-Dump is a simple and fast plugin for WordPress blogs to upload directly their iPhone photos.
Once its installed you can provide a personal username and password. 
At your iPhone you will need to enter:
Username: a username
Password: a password
Domain: domain/wordpressfolder 

== Installation ==

1. Upload `i-Dump` folder into the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Open de i-Dump settings at the admin menu bar.
